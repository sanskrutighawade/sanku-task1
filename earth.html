Few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known astronomical body to harbor life, roughly 4.5 billion years old. It is a rocky terrestrial planet, with 71% of its surface covered by liquid water and a Nitrogen-Oxygen atmosphere that supports life. Earth has one moon and experiences seasons due to its tilt
